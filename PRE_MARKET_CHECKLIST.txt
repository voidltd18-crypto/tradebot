Pre-market checklist

1. Render backend:
   - Deploy successful
   - /status works
   - /version works
   - /search-stocks?q=AMD works
   - Bot ON

2. Vercel frontend:
   - VITE_API_BASE points to Render URL
   - UI shows v1.0-production-stable
   - Reports page opens
   - Search tab returns preview cards

3. Trading safety:
   - Confirm DRY_RUN setting:
     DRY_RUN=true for safe paper/test
     DRY_RUN=false for live money

4. Behaviour expected:
   - Buy same day allowed
   - Sell same day allowed
   - Sold ticker locked until next day
   - Bot moves on to other universe stocks

5. First hour:
   - Watch positions
   - Watch PDT/log warnings
   - Do not change code during active market unless needed
